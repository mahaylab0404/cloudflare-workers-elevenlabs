# CayesDesk Site Audit

Scope: current checked-out React/Vite source in `CayesDeskThisOne`, with desktop spot checks against `http://localhost:3002`. Mobile impressions are inferred from the responsive source and first-viewport content order.

Page: Home (`/`)
Hero first impression (desktop, 3 seconds): The product is clearly about missed consult recovery, but the first viewport feels like a dark SaaS pitch with an overlaid dashboard card instead of a premium clinic brand moment. The headline is strong, but the subhead and CTAs fall below the fold at common laptop heights.
Hero first impression (mobile, 3 seconds): Mostly dark text content and brand chrome before the visual proof arrives. The first scroll likely feels more like a sales page than a luxury medical/wellness concierge experience.
Word density rating: Too high
Visual richness rating: Sparse
Alignment with "missed consult recovery for premium cosmetic clinics": Partial
Top 3 changes this page needs:
- Replace the dark dashboard-led hero with an image-led premium clinic scene and keep headline, subhead, and primary CTA visible on mobile.
- Turn missed revenue into a simple visual math moment early in the page instead of explaining the leak in paragraphs.
- Cut repeated CTA, pricing, signal, and feature-copy sections into one tighter conversion path.
What to preserve:
- Missed consult revenue recovery positioning.
- Live demo line as the fastest proof mechanism.
- 7-day pilot offer and Month 1 credit.
- BAA/security/no-clinical-advice trust cues, but moved lower and made quieter.
